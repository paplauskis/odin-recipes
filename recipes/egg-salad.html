Simple Egg Salad

Description

The simplest, good egg salad that is still delicious! Great for leftover Easter eggs!

Ingredients

  • 6 hard cooked eggs
  • quarter of a cup chopped cucumber
  • 3 tablespoons ranch dressing
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • salt and ground black pepper for taste

Steps

  1. Combine eggs, cucumber, ranch dressing, mustard, salt, and black pepper in a bowl.
